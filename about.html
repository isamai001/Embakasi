About Mr. & Miss Embakasi

Celebrating Talent, Beauty, and Community Spirit

Our Mission

Mr. & Miss Embakasi is committed to discovering, nurturing, and celebrating the unique talents and modeling potential within the Embakasi community. Our mission is to:

  • Empower aspiring models and performers to build confidence and stage presence
  • Offer a platform to showcase talent in modeling, fashion, dance, and the arts
  • Celebrate the beauty, creativity, and culture of the Embakasi youth
  • Support personal branding and career development in the fashion and entertainment industries

Our History

2018 - The Beginning

Founded by a group of community leaders, the first Mr. & Miss Embakasi competition was held with just 20 contestants.

2020 - Going Digital

Launched our online voting platform, allowing the entire community to participate in selecting their favorites.

2022 - Event Collaborations

Formed strategic partnerships with local organizations to host impactful events, giving contestants more exposure and engaging the wider Embakasi community.

2025 - Bigger Than Ever

Mark your calendars! The much-anticipated grand finale of Mr. & Miss Embakasi 2025 will take place this December at the iconic East African School of Aviation Grounds.

Frequently Asked Questions

The competition is open to all residents of Embakasi aged 18-30. Contestants must provide the required information and concent to the submission of personal details as stated required by the privacy policy. Both men and women can participate in their respective categories.
Voting is done through our secure online platform. Each vote costs 20 KSH via M-Pesa. You can vote for multiple contestants, but only once per contestant per day. The voting period runs for 4 weeks leading up to the grand finale.
Winners receive cash prizes, modeling contracts, brand ambassador opportunities, and a chance to lead community development projects. The exact prizes vary each year but are always substantial and career-changing.
60% of proceeds go to organizing the event and prizes, 30% supports community projects in Embakasi, and 10% covers administrative costs. We publish annual financial reports for transparency.
